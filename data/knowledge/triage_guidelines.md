# Triage Guidelines (Local Baseline)

- Red: any life-threatening risk, unstable vitals, or major red-flag symptoms.
- Yellow: urgent conditions requiring prompt observation and priority evaluation.
- Green: stable non-urgent outpatient conditions.

Mandatory safety:
- Temperature must be recorded.
- Red-flag conclusions require rule confirmation.
- If key information is missing and risk cannot be excluded, escalate to human triage.
